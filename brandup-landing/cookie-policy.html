1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work, remember your preferences and understand how it is used.

2. Cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary — required for the site and your session to work (e.g. authentication, security). These cannot be switched off.

  • Analytics — help us measure traffic and improve the Service (only with your consent, where required).

3. Managing cookies

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies via our consent banner where shown, and you can delete or block cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how the site works.

4. Third-party cookies

Some features rely on third parties (for example analytics or embedded channel widgets) that may set their own cookies under their respective policies.
